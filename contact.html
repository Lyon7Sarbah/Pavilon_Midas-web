Common Questions

Is my account secure? +

Yes. The platform uses secure authentication systems, encryption, and optional two-factor authentication (2FA) to protect user accounts.

Can I withdraw my funds? +

Withdrawals depend on your selected plan and account activity. You can manage withdrawal requests directly from your dashboard.

How does copy trading work? +

Copy trading allows you to automatically mirror selected trading strategies in your account. Trades are executed in real-time based on the chosen system.

Do I need trading experience? +

No. The platform is designed for both beginners and experienced traders. You can choose to follow strategies, copy trades, or manage investments through structured systems.

What is Pavilion Midas Academy Ltd? +

Pavilion Midas Academy Ltd is a global financial platform offering trading tools, market analysis, copy trading, and managed portfolio access for investors at different experience levels.

How do I get started? +

Create an account, verify your profile, and choose a plan that fits your investment level. Once set up, you can access trading tools and platform features from your dashboard.
